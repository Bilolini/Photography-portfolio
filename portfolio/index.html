About me

Hi, I am David, and welcome to my site. I have been fortunate to start a career by combining two of my favorite passions, food and photography. Having studied nutrition for a number of years, and combining my background in photography, has led me to where I am today.

I specialize in plant based and vegan recipe's as it aligns with my philosophy of nutrition. I also love to take classic recipes and desserts and make them "healthier". I have an amazing wife and children who vet my work extensively.

Besides recipe development and creating exclusive recipe's for clients I also love commercial work for the food industry, where I try to make their products look irresistible!

I do most of my work in my studio for remote work, but I am also available for on location photo shoots as well. Feel free to reach out if there is anything I can do for you.
